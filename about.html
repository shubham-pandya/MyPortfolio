Greetings, I'm Shubham Pandya! I'm a Master's student for human-centered design, currently on an exciting journey as a Teaching Assistant at the University of Regina, Regina. My professional journey began with a Bachelor's in Technology in Computer Engineering from CHARUSAT, India. During my academic years, I delved deep into the world of code, seeking to understand the 'Why' behind every line I wrote. This quest for comprehension ultimately led me to the captivating realm of User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design.

My career blossomed as I honed my craft, collaborating with visionary companies such as Nimblechapps Pvt. Ltd., CloudZon Inc., CyberUpgrade, and Gopi Color Lab. My passion for problem-solving thrived, and I discovered my purpose in creating seamless, user-centric digital experiences.
